列王纪上、下生命读经
　
一九九四年六月夏季训练
　
中文标语
大卫犯了极其粗鄙的罪得罪了神，神虽然在大卫悔改后赦免了他，并赐他一个儿子，成为神所喜爱的，但神为了自己的公义，仍然惩治他。
所罗门因着接受他许多异教妃嫔所带来的许多偶像而亵慢了神，因此神从所罗门的国割去了十个支派，但还留一个支派给他，使大卫在耶路撒冷有灯光。
以色列人离弃神，随从偶像，所以神将他们分散在万国之中，但在祂永远的爱里，必在末后的日子，将他们带回他们列祖之地。
神那么恩待大卫、所罗门、和以色列，乃是为要保持基督家谱的线，使基督得以进到人性里，并保持一班人，叫他们据有以马内利之地为业，使基督得以建立祂在地上的国。
　
英文标语
　
Life－Study of 1 and 2 Kings
　
Summer Training, June 1994
David committed the greatest gross sin against God, and God forgave him after his repentance and gave him a son as God's beloved, although God still disciplined him for the sake of His righteousness.
Solomon insulted God with many idols through his many pagan wives and concubines, and because of this God cut away ten tribes from his kingdom but still left one tribe with him for David to have a lamp in Jerusalem.
The children of Israel forsook God and went after idols, so God dispersed them into all the nations yet, in His eternal love, would bring them back to their fathers' land in the last days.
God's being so gracious to David, Solomon, and Israel was to keep the lineage of Christ's genealogy that Christ might come into humanity and to keep a people to possess the land of Emmanuel that Christ may come to establish His kingdom on the earth.
