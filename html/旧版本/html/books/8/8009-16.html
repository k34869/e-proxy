基督为父用神圣的荣耀所荣耀的结果
　
中文标语
　
基督为父用神圣的荣耀所荣耀的结果
　
一九九六年十一月感恩节特会
基督神性的荣耀，原藏在祂里面，如同藏在一粒麦种之内。
在祂的得荣里，祂这末后的亚当成了赐生命的灵，为着祂神圣的分赐。
在祂的复活里，祂带着人性生为神的长子，为着祂神圣的变化。
借着祂的复活，所有信祂的人都得重生成为神的众子，为着祂神圣的繁增。
　
英文标语
　
The Issue of Christ Being Glorified by the Father with the Divine Glory
　
Thanksgiving Day Conference, November 1996
The glory of Christ's divinity was concealed in Him as in a grain of wheat.
In His glorification He, as the last Adam, became the life－giving Spirit for His divine dispensing.
In His resurrection He was born with His humanity to be the firstborn Son of God for His divine transformation.
Through His resurrection all His believers were regenerated to be the many sons of God for His divine multiplication.
